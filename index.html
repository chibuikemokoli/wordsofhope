About Us

The Words of Hope Trauma Relief Foundation is a nonprofit organization based in Lagos, Nigeria. One of its objectives is working to improve the lives of children/youths through direct service, research and training.

A major activity of the foundation is to translate emerging findings about the human brain and child development into practical interventions for the way we nurture, protect, enrich, educate and heal children of adverse experiences.
