Our Programs for 2023-2024

The Words of Hope Trauma Relief Foundation is a nonprofit organization based in Lagos, Nigeria. A major activity of the foundation is to translate emerging findings about the human brain and child development into practical interventions for the way we nurture, protect, enrich, educate and heal children of adverse experiences.
