Counselling Services

WOHTRF offers individual and couples therapy in Africa and remotely world-wide.

We take an eclectic approach to developing interventions and strongly believe that everyone has the ability to make positive change.


Individual Therapy

Therapy focuses on helping clients learn skills and develop a tool box to address their needs.

Learn More

Couples Therapy

We provide short term, targeted couples counseling that includes an in depth assessment of your relationship with the goal of improving communication, increasing intimacy, and developing positive behaviors that support the relationship.

Learn More

Teletherapy

We offer remote therapy sessions over a HIPAA compliant secure video platform for clients in New York and Virginia. Teletherapy is convenient and confidential.
